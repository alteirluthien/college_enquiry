Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our college programs, admissions, and services.

What are the entry requirements?

+

Entry requirements vary depending on the course. Generally, you need:

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA requirements (varies by program)
  • English proficiency test scores (for international students)
  • Program-specific prerequisites

Please check the specific program page or contact admissions for detailed requirements.

How do I apply for an undergraduate program?

+

To apply for an undergraduate program:

  1. Create an account on our admission portal
  2. Complete the online application form
  3. Upload required documents (transcripts, test scores, etc.)
  4. Pay the application fee
  5. Submit your application before the deadline

Application deadlines vary by program, so check early!

How much are the course fees?

+

Course fees vary by program and student type:

  • Engineering Programs: $15,000/year
  • Business Programs: $12,000/year
  • Arts & Sciences: $10,000/year
  • Graduate Programs: $18,000/year

Financial aid and scholarships are available. Contact our financial aid office for more information.

How do I pay my fees?

+

You can pay your fees through multiple methods:

  • Online via student portal (credit card, debit card)
  • Bank transfer
  • Payment plans (available for full-time students)
  • In-person at the bursar's office

Payment deadlines are strictly enforced. Late payments may result in additional fees.

Do you offer scholarships?

+

Yes! We offer various scholarship opportunities:

  • Merit-based scholarships: For students with outstanding academic performance
  • Need-based scholarships: For students with financial need
  • Program-specific scholarships: Available for certain fields of study
  • International scholarships: For international students

Scholarship applications are typically due with your admission application. Check our financial aid page for more details.
